About AFDS

Aboriginal Front Door Society(AFDS) is a culturally safe, peer-designed non-judgemental place for Aboriginal Peoples their friends, and family in the Downtown East Side. They create hope and offer support through Aboriginal cultural traditions, caring, and acceptance.

So when I knew that I could choose either an NGO or a local business website to redesign for our final project, I decided to go for Aboriginal Front Door Society (AFDS).
