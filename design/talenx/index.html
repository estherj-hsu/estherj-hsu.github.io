Case 1: Role Builder

What’s this for?

Let users to search a particular job role by keyword and set it as the main role, then attach at the top of 5 sub-roles to the main role if needed.

When choosing a ready made job profile, they can first search via our search bar placed in the middle of the screen instead of the top.

After searching for a job title, the relevant jobs are displayed in the bottom to show the most closely related jobs to choose from.

This will give opportunity to client to specify their job, or create a new one from scratch.

We will have this grouping ready for system.

What I think?

According to the reference, I got:

  1. It’s a two-step flow selector
  2. The list of roles are in “groups”
