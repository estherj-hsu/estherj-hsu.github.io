Small changes, but they make the site feel calmer and more intentional.

Portfolio updates 💼

I updated the portfolio page to include some of the more recent projects I’ve been working on at work, and restructured it to separate Featured Personal Projects from client-facing Front-End Projects.

It feels clearer now, both for myself and for anyone browsing the work.
