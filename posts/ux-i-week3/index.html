The Affinity Diagram(Map)

A visual tool that helps you organize the information you produce during a brainstorming session

WHEN

  • After brainstorming with a large group of people
  • You’re solving a complex problem
  • Having a large number of ideas or lots of data to work with
  • You’re eager to think outside the box

WHY

  • Make it easier to develop an action plan for a large number of ideas
  • Help define problems and develop potential ideas for solutions
  • Go from analysis to synthesis

Communication principles

Organizing your site content: useful, effective, and well-written

Guidelines

  • Start with critical content: What are the core questions people have when visiting your site?
  • Group together related content: As a result, navigating your website becomes more intuitive and more efficient
  • Show only what is relevant: Keep it short and simple
  • Show everything that is relevant: Empathy. Whit is a mistake to be repetitive and redundant make sure you do not leave
  • Consider differences within your target audience: Start with the most relevant user.
  • Test before implementation

Site mapping

  • Establishing sections of the website and navigation
  • Explaining relationships between sections or content
  • Enabling designers and developers to see duplicate sections or gaps in content

UI is communication

  • Be polite. Be respectful. Be logical.
  • The objective is to have the same standards for website interaction as we do for social interaction.
  • Be purposeful.
  • Be easy to understand.
  • Be timely.
  • Have a pleasant personality and tone
  • Rarely interrupt
  • Consistency is key.

Finding your voice

  • Voice
    Consistent brand personality your organization uses in all its communications, includes word choices, pronouns, punctuation, and more.
  • Tone
    How you communicate and the attitude you convey depending on the topic or situation and the impression it leaves on your visitors.
  • Style Guidelines to ensure you present a uniform brand experience using defined language choices and writing mechanics.
  • Identify who you’re talking to
  • Gathering and re-writing content
  • Try to define your brand voice in three words
  • Write content down and revisit it often
